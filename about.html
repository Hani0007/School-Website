About Us

At Covenant Zion Place, we are dedicated to nurturing young minds and fostering a love for learning within a safe.

    we have been committed to providing a holistic education that prepares our students for success in both their academic and personal pursuits
  • Our mission at Covenant Zion School is to empower each student to achieve their full potential and become responsible global citizens.
  • At Covenant Zion School, we combine a rigorous academic curriculum with a focus on character development and critical thinking skills.

Our state-of-the-art facilities provide students with a conducive learning environment that supports their academic and extracurricular activities.

We welcome you to become a part of our vibrant community at Covenant Zion School, where we strive to inspire a lifelong passion for learning and foster the leaders of tomorrow.

We celebrate diversity and promote an environment of mutual respect, understanding, and inclusivity among our students, staff, and the community.
