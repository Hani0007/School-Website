Humanities And Arts

The Humanities and Arts program at Covenant Zion place is designed to foster a deep appreciation for cultural diversity, creativity, and critical thinking, nurturing students' intellectual, emotional, and artistic development.We offer a comprehensive humanities curriculum that includes literature, history, philosophy, and social sciences.

Experimental Education

Our educational approach is centered on a student-centered learning model that encourages active participation, critical thinking, and a holistic understanding of the subjects.Opportunities for practical, hands-on learning experiences to encourage real-world application of theoretical knowledge and skill development.

Character education at Covenant Zion School is an integral part of our holistic approach to nurturing well-rounded individuals with strong moral character, empathy, and a sense of social responsibility.
